Shipping & Returns

We aim to deliver your orders quickly and efficiently while ensuring a smooth return experience.

Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location.

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days
  • Free shipping on orders above ₹999

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking details.

Return Policy

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused and in original condition.

  • Easy return request via account page
  • Refund processed within 5-7 business days
  • Exchange available for size issues

Contact Support

For any shipping or return queries, contact us at support@aesivo.com.
